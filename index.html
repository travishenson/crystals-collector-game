Crystals Collector

Rules:

  1. You will be given a random number at the start of the game as your target score.
  2. There are four crystals. By clicking on a crystal, you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.
  3. You win the game by matching your total score to the target score; you lose the game if your total score goes above the target score.
  4. The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click on it.
  5. Each time the game starts, the game will change the values of each crystal.
  6. If you wish to reset the game's wins and losses, click the button below. This will also randomize the target and crystal numbers.
